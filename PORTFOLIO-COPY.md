## Project 1: DealQuary - SaaS Deal Intelligence Platform

**Title:** DealQuary
**Subtitle:** Multi-Deal SaaS Subscription Calculator

**Description:**
A financial intelligence platform that empowers SaaS sales teams to model complex subscription deals with precision. DealQuary calculates MRR, ARR, contract terms, discounts, CAC/LTV ratios, and generates professional statements of work—all in a modern, intuitive interface.

**Problem Solved:**
Eliminates spreadsheet chaos in B2B SaaS sales by providing a centralized, error-proof deal calculator that ensures accurate pricing and revenue forecasting across multiple contracts.

**Technologies Used:**
- Next.js 15 (App Router, Server Components)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Prisma ORM + PostgreSQL
- Stripe integration for billing
- Real-time calculation engine

**Key Features:**
- Multi-deal management with sidebar navigation
- Live MRR/ARR calculations with discount modeling
- CAC/LTV analytics dashboard
- Statement of Work (SOW) generator
- Responsive design with glassmorphic UI
- Role-based access control

**Summary:**
Built a production SaaS platform that transforms complex subscription pricing into clear, actionable financial intelligence for sales teams—featuring real-time calculations, multi-deal tracking, and automated SOW generation.

**Technologies Used:**
